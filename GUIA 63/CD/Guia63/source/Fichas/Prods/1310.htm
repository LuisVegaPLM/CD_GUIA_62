Dual-Bay CR Mammography system - ChromeDuoTM

DESCRIPCIÓN/DESCRIPTION:

At iCRco, durability and reliability are the cornerstones of our engineering philosophy. Our innovations in CR and DR radiography have produced advancements such as True Flat Scan Path™, proprietary technology that extends the life of the CR cassette by completely eliminating the plate handling process; and ICE-M, an image clarity enhancement algorithm specifically designed for mammography applications.

Introducing the ChromeDuo™ CR mammography system - the next evolution in iCRco digital imaging technology. The ChromeDuo incorporates a dual-bay cassette system and combines it with an ultra high- resolution scan sensor, making it the only CR system in the industry that can simultaneously scan and process two images at the same time. All without sacrificing image quality or time.

CARACTERÍSTICAS/CHARACTERISTICS:

• Dual-bay cassette system for fast image processing.

• Ultra-precise and high resolution scanning.

• True Flat Scan Path™ technology.

The ChromeDuo is paired with XC™, iCRco’s superior image acquisition software. Custom settings can be defined based on user preference, allowing you to apply advanced image processing algorithms to look for even the smallest details.

Add our complete mammorgraphy workflow solution and you have the whole package - image acquisition, RIS-PACS, a desktop viewer, a mobile viewer for the iPad™ or iPhone™, an incomparable image wizard, and a comprehensive QC tool. It’s mammography as you’ve never seen it before.
